What is Tōdai?

Tōdai (灯台) encompasses a lifestyle philosophy that encourages self-reflection and action to improve ones life. Translated from Japanese, Tōdai means "lighthouse" and encompasses our ideal as a beacon of focus in a turbulent sea of distractions.

The Tōdai platform is designed as a private online application that allows you to choose important values and evaluate how your lifestyle aligns with those values.

How it works!

Choose a Focus

Choose 3 - 5 values that you wish to incorporate into your daily routine

Live your Life

Go about your day and make choices with your desired values in mind

...and Reflect
